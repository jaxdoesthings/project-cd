Program Information

Software Developer I

  • Phase 1: Pre-work – Virtual Introduction to Coding Basics
  • Phase 2: Training – Immersive Workforce Development
  • Online tutorials, instructor-led sessions, hands-on lab & project work, professional development, and more!
  • On the job training

Requirements

  • Must be 18 years of age or older
  • Must have High School Diploma/GED or higher
  • Must be a Delaware resident
  • Must be able to permanently work in the United States without requiring sponsorship at any time
  • Must be currently unemployed or underemployed (see FAQ)
  • Must be impacted by COVID-19 (see FAQ)
Future requirements may be subject to change
